# Awesome LLM Persuasion

A curated reading list on persuasion and large language models (LLMs). As LLMs become increasingly capable of generating persuasive text, a growing body of research asks: how persuasive are these models, how do we measure it, and what are the implications? This list brings together work on evaluation frameworks and benchmarks, comparisons with human persuasion, strategic and theory-of-mind approaches, personalization and targeting, safety risks including manipulation and propaganda, and domain applications in politics, health, and marketing.
